Starter checklist

A simple path for students who are overwhelmed and do not know where to begin.

Step 1

Prepare documents

Keep your passport, visa, I-20, admission letter, and SEVIS payment record in one folder.

Step 2

Connect with Wright State

Review orientation guidance, UCIE instructions, and billing or registration support.

Step 3

Update after arrival

Check your I-94, attend required sessions, confirm classes, and ask questions early.

Step 4

Maintain status

Know travel rules, address updates, enrollment requirements, and employment limits.

Step 5

Build daily life systems

Set up housing, food, transportation, payment methods, and your weekly class-work routine.

Support and safety

Best practice for immigration questions

If an answer affects your status, travel, work eligibility, or documents, confirm it with Wright State UCIE and the related official government site.
